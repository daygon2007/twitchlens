What is TwitchLens?

TwitchLens is a web application that lets you enter your expected Twitch channel viewer count and suggests games that may bring you more viewers on Twitch! The hope is that you, the Twitch streamer, can choose games intelligently based on how many viewers you expect to get. It is perfect for variety streamers on Twitch!

Why do I care about this data?

If you play games no one is watching, it's unlikely you will find new viewers on Twitch. Also, if you play games that too many popular streamers are playing, you will rank so low no one will find you.

So which games should I play?

Play games that many people are watching

On Twitch's Game Directory page, games are listed by total site viewer count. Games that are popular show at the top of this page, and are more likely to be clicked by more viewers.

Play games that have no/few streamers playing that are more popular than you

On each individual game's directory page (example: Dark Souls) each channel is listed by viewer count. Channels that have less viewers are pushed far down the list.

If you can be one of the top 3 streamers in one of the top 25 games on Twitch, that is best. Get as close to that as you can.
